## What ScooterNav Is

**ScooterNav** is a production-ready, cross-platform Progressive Web App (PWA)
built specifically for electric scooter navigation. It fills a gap not currently
addressed by general-purpose navigation platforms (Google Maps, Waze, Apple Maps,
HERE, TomTom) by offering:

| Feature | ScooterNav | Google Maps | Waze | Apple Maps |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dedicated e-scooter routing | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Bike lane classification (protected/shared/none) | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| EV charging station proximity alerts | ✅ | Limited | ❌ | ❌ |
| Speed-based ETA (8–22 mph scooter profiles) | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| 9-language voice navigation | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Offline tile caching (2,000 tiles) | ✅ | ❌ (paid) | ❌ | ❌ |
| PWA — no app store required | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| Safety score per route | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

## Why This Matters for Your Platform

The global electric scooter market is projected to exceed **$42 billion by 2030**
(Grand View Research). As micro-mobility grows, navigation platforms that lack
scooter-specific routing are increasingly uncompetitive in that segment.

ScooterNav's core intellectual property — including its **geodesic routing
engine (Haversine)**, **lane-awareness model**, **multi-modal speed profiles**,
and **scooter-specific safety scoring** — is immediately licensable and
integrable into existing mapping products.
