# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

M&S.co ("we", "us", or "our") operates ScooterNav ("the Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Location Data
- **GPS/Location Information**: We collect your device's location data to provide navigation services, find nearby charging stations, and suggest routes.
- This data is used in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers.

### 1.2 Usage Data
- Navigation preferences (speed settings, lane preferences)
- Language preferences
- Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
- Saved locations (Home, Work, etc.)

### 1.3 Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- Browser type (for web version)
- Screen resolution

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide navigation and routing services
- Show nearby charging stations
- Deliver voice navigation instructions
- Remember your preferences
- Improve the Application's functionality

## 3. Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Most preferences are stored locally on your device
- **No Account Required**: We do not require user accounts or collect personal identification information
- **No Tracking**: We do not track your location history or store your travel patterns

## 4. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information:

- **OpenStreetMap**: For map data and tiles
- **Leaflet**: For map rendering

These services have their own privacy policies governing data collection.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your location data with advertisers
- Provide your data to third parties for marketing purposes

We MAY share information:
- When required by law
- To protect our rights or safety
- With service providers who assist in operating the Application (under strict confidentiality agreements)

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your locally stored data through your device settings
- Delete the Application and all associated local data
- Disable location services at any time through your device settings

## 9. Offline Use

When using the Application offline:
- Only locally cached data is used
- No information is transmitted to external servers

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the Application or on our GitHub repository.
